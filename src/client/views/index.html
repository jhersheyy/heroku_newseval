Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment Analysis uses Natural Language Processing (see below) to find the overall feeling in a block of text, such as a blog post, tweet, yelp review,etc. Sentiment analysis is also known as opinion mining or emotion AI. A basic example is to determine the overall polarity of a text, whether it is more positive or negative. While there are various methods to perform sentiment analysis, most of them involve breaking the text to be analyzed into smaller chunks like sentences or phrases. These smaller chunks are then analyzed and scored consistently, so the overall text is just the scores of the smaller chunks combined. For example, if a text has 3 phrases, and 2 are positive, but 1 is negative, then the text will be positive overall.
